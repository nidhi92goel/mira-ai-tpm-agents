# Project Plan – AI Adoption for ABCDE Ltd.

## Executive Summary
This project aims to enable AI-driven efficiencies across ABCDE Ltd. by implementing intelligent automation in key business workflows. The initiative focuses on improving operational productivity, reducing manual effort, and enabling data-driven decision-making. The project will be delivered in structured phases, ensuring minimal disruption to existing systems.

---

## Business Objective
The objective is to successfully integrate AI capabilities into existing business processes to enhance efficiency, reduce costs, and improve scalability. The solution will focus on automation, predictive insights, and improved workflow management.

---

## Project Scope

### In Scope
- AI-based workflow automation  
- Integration with existing systems  
- Data processing and analytics  
- Training internal teams on AI tools  

### Out of Scope
- Full system overhaul  
- Replacement of core legacy systems  
- Custom AI model training from scratch
